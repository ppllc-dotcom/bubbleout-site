bubbleOut Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Summary

bubbleOut does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers. The app has no internet access permission and contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services of any kind.

Where your data lives

All app settings — your screen-time limit, your preferences, and your day-to-day tracked screen time — are stored locally on your device only, using standard Android app storage. None of it is ever transmitted anywhere, because the app has no network access at all.

Permissions bubbleOut uses, and why

  • Display over other apps — required to show the floating reminder bubbles.
  • Foreground service — keeps the screen-time tracker running reliably in the background.
  • Post notifications (Android 13+) — required to show the ongoing tracking status notification.

Third parties

bubbleOut does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries. No data is collected, so none is shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app's features evolve — for example, if a future version adds an advertising integration, this page will be updated to reflect exactly what that involves before that update is released.
